Our Neurology Services

We provide comprehensive care for a wide range of neurological disorders with the latest diagnostic tools and treatment options.

Epilepsy & Seizure Disorders

Our comprehensive epilepsy program offers advanced diagnostic testing including video EEG monitoring, neuropsychological testing, and specialized imaging.

We provide personalized treatment plans that may include medication management, dietary therapy (ketogenic diet), neurostimulation devices (VNS, RNS), and surgical evaluation when appropriate.

Our team includes epileptologists, neurosurgeons, neuropsychologists, and specialized nurses who work together to optimize seizure control and quality of life.

Stroke & Cerebrovascular Care

As a certified stroke center, we provide 24/7 emergency stroke care with rapid evaluation and treatment including tPA administration and endovascular thrombectomy.

Our stroke specialists focus on acute intervention, secondary prevention, and rehabilitation to maximize recovery. Services include advanced neuroimaging, carotid ultrasound, and specialized stroke clinics.

We participate in cutting-edge stroke research and clinical trials to bring our patients the latest treatment options.

Memory Disorders & Dementia

Our memory disorders program offers comprehensive evaluation for Alzheimer's disease and other dementias using advanced cognitive testing, biomarker analysis, and genetic counseling.

We provide personalized treatment plans including medication management, lifestyle interventions, caregiver support, and access to clinical trials of promising new therapies.

Our multidisciplinary team includes neurologists, neuropsychologists, social workers, and therapists who specialize in dementia care.
